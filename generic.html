About Me

Hi there! My name is Melodie and I'm a Data Analyst and budding Data Scientist. My journey with data started back in my unidays, where I kept a financial record of all my expenses in my mobile excel app, analyzed the data I had collected and then gained insights on how to properly manage my spendings.

This experience opened my eyes to the importance of data, and how having the right data could solve a lot of problems. I went on to get my first job as a part-time Data Analyst and the rest, they say, is history.

Core Skills:
Language: Python(Numpy, Pandas, ScikitLearn, Matplotlib, seaborn)
Cloud Skills: Azure(kubernetes, Docker), AWS(SageMaker, S3, EC2)
Data Visualization: (PowerBI, Tableau)
Database: SQL(MS SQL, PostgreSQL)

I know you're probably wondering how I got into data science, because I started off talking about Data Analysis and now most of the skills I listed are data science skills. Well, the motivating factor for getting into data science is my love for technology and coding. As a Data Analyst I didn't get to do any coding, but once I started coding I knew it was something I'd love to keep doing, so that, coupled with my love for data, getting into Data science was a no-brainer.

When I'm not on my computer, I love watching Chinese movies and animes, learning new languages, and playing high resolution games. My goal is to be a valuable asset to any team, and in the long term make significant impact in my country using Data. Hopefully we cross paths in the real world someday and we can make it a better place together.
